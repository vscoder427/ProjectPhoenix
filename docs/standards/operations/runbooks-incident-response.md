# Runbooks and Incident Response Standard

This standard defines operational runbooks and incident response requirements.

## Runbook Scope

- Full runbook required per service
- Includes deploy, rollback, alert response, escalation, and post-mortem steps

## Severity Levels

- Sev0 to Sev4 with response time expectations

## Response Process

- Incident checklist and comms templates required
- Timeline log required for all incidents
- Post-incident review required

## Post-Mortems

- Required for Sev0, Sev1, and Sev2 incidents

## On-Call

- Formal on-call rotation with escalation policy
