# Cost Management Standard

This standard defines cost visibility and control.

## Budgets

- Per-service budgets with monthly thresholds
- Alert on 80% and 100% budget usage

## Cost Anomaly Detection

- Weekly review of cost anomalies
- Tag all services for cost attribution

## Optimization

- Quarterly cost optimization review
- Document monthly budget reviews and anomalies in the [Service Ownership Checklist](../templates/service-ownership-checklist.md).
