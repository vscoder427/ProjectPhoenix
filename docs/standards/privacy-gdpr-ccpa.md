# Privacy (GDPR/CCPA) Standard

This standard defines privacy requirements for services that handle personal data.

## Scope

- Applies to all PII regardless of location
- Covers GDPR and CCPA/CPRA requirements

## Lawful Basis and Consent

- Document lawful basis for all personal data processing
- Consent must be explicit where required and recorded with timestamps
- Provide opt-out for sale/sharing of personal data (CCPA/CPRA)

## Data Subject Rights (DSAR)

- Support access, correction, deletion, and portability requests
- GDPR response deadline: 30 days
- CCPA response deadline: 45 days
- Maintain DSAR logs and verification of completion

## Data Minimization and Purpose Limitation

- Collect only necessary personal data
- Do not reuse personal data outside stated purposes without approval

## Breach Notification

- GDPR notification within 72 hours of discovery
- CCPA notification per applicable state timelines

## Vendor and Transfers

- DPAs required for vendors processing personal data
- Document cross-border transfers and safeguards
