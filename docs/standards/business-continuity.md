# Business Continuity Standard

This standard defines disaster recovery and continuity requirements.

## Backup and Restore

- Automated daily backups for all data stores
- Quarterly restore drills

## RTO/RPO

- RTO: 4 hours for critical services
- RPO: 1 hour for critical data

## Regional Resilience

- Document regional dependencies
- Plan for multi-region failover

## Incident Escalation

- Continuity plan linked in runbooks
