# HIPAA Risk Analysis Standard

This standard defines the required HIPAA risk analysis process and artifacts.

## Required Artifacts

- System-wide PHI data flow diagram
- Risk analysis report with identified risks and mitigations
- Risk management plan with owners and timelines
- Annual review schedule

## Scope

- All services handling or transmitting PHI
- All vendors that process or store PHI

## Minimum Requirements

- Identify where PHI is collected, stored, transmitted, and logged
- Identify threats and vulnerabilities
- Rate likelihood and impact
- Define mitigation actions and timelines
